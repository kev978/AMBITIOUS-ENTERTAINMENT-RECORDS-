The Notorious B.I.G – Life After Death

Price: P320.00

“Life After Death” is the second and final studio album by The Notorious B.I.G, released in 1997. A monumental double album, it showcases Biggie’s storytelling, charisma, and versatility with iconic tracks like “Hypnotize,” “Mo Money Mo Problems,” and “Ten Crack Commandments.” It remains one of the most influential hip-hop albums ever recorded.
